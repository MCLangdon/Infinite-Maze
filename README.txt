To play the game, run Infinite-Maze/Builds/Assignment1.exe. Use WASD/arrows to move, mouse to rotate, spacebar to jump, 'f' to fire projectiles. Player cannot move laterally while jumping. Enter the maze by moving through the opening in the outer wall. Run through orange projectiles to pick them up. Fire 3 projectiles at an inner maze wall to destroy it. Fire a projectile outside of the maze or at an outer maze wall to terminate generation of the maze. Run through the yellow arch to win the game.
